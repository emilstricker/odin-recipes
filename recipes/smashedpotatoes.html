Smashed Potatoes

Description

Use the microwave to create a stunning dish with feta, sweet potatoes, coriander and some jalapeños as well!

Ingredients

  • Jalapeños
  • Potatoes
  • Sweet Potatoes
  • Feta
  • Olive Oil
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Chop everything
  2. Mix together
  3. Microwave for 12 minutes
  4. Drizzle olive oil
  5. Serve
